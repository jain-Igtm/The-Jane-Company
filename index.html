About the company

A name on the door.

The Jane Company is not a corporation so much as a sign hung over a private workshop: a place to gather the work that would otherwise stay scattered across apps, notes, drafts, voice memos, exported files, and half-finished folders.

The style is old without being dusty, simple without being empty, and built so the next update can add real posts instead of rebuilding the whole house.
